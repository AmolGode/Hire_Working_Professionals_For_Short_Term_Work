About Page

For individual working professionals like electricians, painters, makeup artists, etc online advertisement or maintaining their website is not affordable. And also for a person, finding the best working professional according to their need is a very time-consuming task. Short-Term-Hire is a website that provides a common platform for a person who wants to hire a working professional and a working professional who want to get hired for a short-term job. In short on our website, a person can search/hire Working Professionals according to their needs for a short-term job.
